Coffee


Sustenance of university life

Description

The daily driver for 92% of university students (me included).
Cheap, quick, and gets the job done (at the expense of your health of course. Nothing in life comes easy)

Ingredients

  • Hot water
  • A stick of coffee

Steps

  1. Open the stick of coffee and pour it on a mug
  2. Add boiling water
  3. Stir and enjoy
